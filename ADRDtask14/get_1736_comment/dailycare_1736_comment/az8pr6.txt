Yes, it can be a sign of dementia but take her to an audiologist before jumping straight to Alzheimer's.  Our brains will try to make patterns out of chaos, and if she has hearing loss it might present like faint music or conversations.  [Musical Ear Syndrome](http://hearinglosshelp.com/blog/apophenia-audio-pareidolia-and-musical-ear-syndrome/)
Certainly something worth investigating.
