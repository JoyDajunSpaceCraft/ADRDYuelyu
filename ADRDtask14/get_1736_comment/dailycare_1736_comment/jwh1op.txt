Ask to meet with the dementia team to request what opportunities will be in place for your mother's social engagement and possibly to meet her physical needs if and when her mobility becomes an issue.
Visit *all* of them.  Look for engaged and well-cared for residents.  Choose a place where you can easily visit.  Check reviews online and use services like Medicare's [Care Compare](https://www.medicare.gov/care-compare/) to check ratings.  I'm sure other countries have similar services.
